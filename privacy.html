Privacy Policy

F1 Dashboard - New Tab Extension

Last Updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

F1 Dashboard ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Data Collection

F1 Dashboard does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

We do not:

  • Collect any personally identifiable information
  • Track your browsing history
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Send any data to external servers (except for fetching F1 race data)
  • Share any information with third parties

3. Data Usage

The extension uses data in the following ways:

  • F1 Race Data: The extension fetches publicly available Formula 1 data from the OpenF1 API (https://api.openf1.org) to display race information, standings, and schedules.
  • User Preferences: Your favorite driver and team selections are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Analytics: We do not use any analytics, tracking, or monitoring tools.

4. Permissions Explanation

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage: Used to save your favorite driver and team preferences locally on your device.
  • Host Permissions (api.openf1.org): Required to fetch live Formula 1 race data, standings, and calendar information from the OpenF1 API.

5. Third-Party Services

The extension uses the OpenF1 API to retrieve publicly available Formula 1 race data. When you use the extension, your browser makes requests to api.openf1.org to fetch this information. Please refer to OpenF1's privacy policy for information about how they handle API requests.

6. Data Security

All user preferences (favorite driver and team) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage API. This data is never transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension is safe for users of all ages.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

9. Disclaimer

This extension is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Formula 1, FIA, or any official F1 entities. All trademarks and logos belong to their respective owners. Race data is provided by the OpenF1 API.
